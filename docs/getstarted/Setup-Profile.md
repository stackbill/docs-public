## Setting up user profile in CloudPortal CMP

**CloudPortal CMP**'s Profile feature allows users to change their password and set up payment settings for their convenience. In addition, users can view their credits, promocodes. The Profile feature in [**CloudPortal CMP**](https://www.CloudPortal.com/)  provides a comprehensive view of a user's account.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Userprofile1-CloudPortalCloudManagementportal.png" width="80%" />

- Once you enter into the **CloudPortal** portal, you will see the **My Profile option**, where you can update your information and upload images.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Userprofile2-CloudPortalCloudManagementportal.png" width="80%" />

- **User credit limit** **(a limit that the admin allocates to the user to use the resource)** - in this option, the user can view their admin-allocated credit limit.
- Once every **changes got completed**, click the **Save Changes** button.

## Change password for User Account in CloudPortal Portal

- To **change your account password**, click on the **Change Password option** located under My Profile.
- **Password change** menu apperas on the screen.
- To change your account password, click on the **Change Password** option placed under My Profile.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Changepassword1-CloudPortalCloudManagement.png" width="100%" />

- Now Change Password menu appears on the screen.
- **Enter the Old Password** and then **enter the New Password** and confirm it.
- Then, Click on the Update button.
- Now the **New Password has been updated successfully**.

## Choose your Payment Method in User Account

- To **Select your payment**, click on the **Payment setting option** placed under the Change Password option.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Payment-CloudPortalCloudManagementPortal.png" width="80%" />

- **Payment settings** menu apperas on the screen.
- Select the **required payment gateways**

- It is possible to integrate the **user's own country's payment gateway** into the **CloudPortal CloudStack Billing Portal**, but this should be communicated to **CloudPortal Admin** beforehand.

:::info
**The CloudPortal CloudStack Billing Portal can be customised to accept payments through the user's home country's payment gateway, however this must first be approved by CloudPortal Admin.**
:::

## Credits & Promocode for User in CloudPortal CMP

- Under the **Payment setting option**, click on **Credits & Promo codes** to check your credit limits and offers.
- Now the Credits & Promo code option appears on the screen.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Credits&promocode-CloudPortalCloudManagementPortal.png" width="100%" />

- Total Credit Limit will display the number of credits available for the user.
- Under the Used Credit Limit, the user can view their used credit limit.
- Balance Available Credit Limit will display the available credit limits.

## API Access Settings in CloudPortal CMP

- Under the **Credit & Promocode**, click on **Request API Access option** to access API setting.
- Now **API Access** option appears on the screen.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/APIaccess1-CloudPortalCloudManagementPortal.png" width="90%" />

- **CloudPortal API** allows you to **manage** your **Instances** and **other resources** programmatically using **conventional HTTP request**.
- **CloudPortal's API** allows you to perform any action you can perform through the Control Panel (except creating personal access tokens).

## Notification Settings in CloudPortal CMP

- Under the **Manage API Access**, click on **Notification Settings** option to set notification alerts.
- Now **Notification Settings** option appears on the screen.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Notificatisettings1-CloudPortalCloudManagementportal.png" width="80%" />

- **Maintenance and outage notification** - To receive notifications about outages and maintenance, the user can enable the Outage and maintenance notification option.
- **Invoice prefrence** - When the invoice reminder option is enabled, the user will receive a notification about the bill payment three days before the end of the month.
- **Payments Overdue** - By enabling the Payment Overdue option, users can receive notifications regarding overdue payments.

## The process of removing an account from the CloudPortal CMP

- Under the **Notification settings**, click on **Access Delete Request** option to delete account from the portal.
- Now Account delete request screen appears.

<img src="/img/gettingstarted/Deleteaccount-CloudPortalCMP.png" width="80%" />

- Kindly follow the procedure to **delete your account** from **CloudPortal.**
